title Buy Milk - Activity Diagram

start

:Buyer opens buy milk page;
:Browse farms and choose milk type;
:Enter quantity and preferred session;

if (All details valid?) then (yes)
  :Create milk order request;
  :Send order to farm owner;

  if (Owner accepts order?) then (yes)
    :Confirm order;
    :Show order confirmed message;
    :Buyer views confirmed status;
  else (no)
    :Mark order as rejected;
    :Show order rejected message;
    :Buyer views rejected status;
  endif

else (no)
  :Show error: please fill all required details;
endif

stop
